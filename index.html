About DAVETEG Schools

Established in 1998, DAVETEG Nursery and Primary School has a rich history of delivering high-quality education rooted in strong values and discipline. Located in the heart of the community, we provide a nurturing environment aimed at developing confident, innovative, and responsible learners.

Our mission is to mould the young minds of today to become the leaders and visionaries of tomorrow. We cherish diversity, encourage creativity, and foster an atmosphere where every child’s talents and abilities can flourish.

At DAVETEG, we believe education is more than academics — we focus on the all-round development of the child encompassing social skills, emotional intelligence, moral compass, and physical fitness.

Curriculum

Our carefully tailored curriculum strikes a perfect balance between academic rigor and creative exploration. We adopt the Nigerian National Curriculum integrated with global best practices to prepare students for the future.

  • Core Subjects: Mathematics, English Language, Science, Social Studies
  • Creative Arts: Drawing, Music, Drama
  • Physical Education: Daily fitness and sports activities
  • Information Technology: Basic computer literacy starting from Nursery
  • Religious and Moral Education: Emphasizing good values and social responsibility

Regular assessments, project-based learning, and interactive lessons ensure learners understand concepts deeply and apply knowledge confidently.

Admission Process

We welcome applications from parents seeking quality education and a secure, child-friendly atmosphere. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

  • Submit completed application form available at the school’s reception or downloadable from our website.
  • Provide photocopies of birth certificate and previous school report (if applicable).
  • Attend an interview with the child for assessment purposes.
  • Pay applicable registration fees to confirm admission.

For enquiries or to schedule a school tour, please contact our admission office.

Our Facilities

DAVETEG School provides a stimulating and safe environment to support effective learning and development through the following facilities:

Spacious Classrooms

Well-ventilated and brightly lit classrooms equipped with comfortable furniture.

Modern Library

A comprehensive collection of books, digital resources, and reading areas to encourage literacy.

Computer Lab

State-of-the-art computer stations providing students digital literacy and coding skills.

Creative Arts Studio

Dedicated art and craft rooms fostering creativity and artistic expression.

Safe, secure play areas and fields for football, athletics, and other sports activities.

Health Clinic

On-site nurse for first aid, regular health checks, and support.

Gated and supervised environment with CCTV and trained security personnel.
